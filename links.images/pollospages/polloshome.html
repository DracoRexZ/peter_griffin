Welcome to

LOS POLLOS HERMANOS

Los Pollos Hermanos is home to Albuquerque's best chicken. Founded by Gustavo Fring and Max Arciniega in 1987, the franchise has expanded vastly over the course of its 20+ year lifespan.

Our number one goal is to provide a satisfactory customer experience, and to deliver delicious meals to the Southwest United States.
